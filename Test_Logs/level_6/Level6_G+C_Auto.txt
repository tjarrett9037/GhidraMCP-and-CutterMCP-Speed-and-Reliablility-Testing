  99 d9 b9 ba 89 de 6e 54 f5 d5 ff ab dd 8d 5f 36
  de d4 9c ad dd 9b 5f 58 99 9a b1
- Logic flow in checker:
  1) copy input to local buffer (thunk at 0x401030)
  2) install/trigger anti-debug behavior:
     - FUN_004043b0(5, FUN_00401725)
     - FUN_00404470(5)
  3) transform local input:
     - FUN_0040179d(local_buf, 0x2b, 0xAABBCCDDEEFF0011)

Interpretation:
- Input length expected: 0x2b = 43 bytes.
- Compare thunk at 0x401070 is memcmp-like; copy thunk at 0x401030 is memcpy/strncpy-like.
- Key constant for transform is 0xAABBCCDDEEFF0011.

Why this mattered:
- Provided enough data to mathematically invert transformation and recover plaintext password.

- FUN_00401725:
  - DAT_004a40d0 = 0
- FUN_00401739:
  - builds 64-bit value from 8 key bytes and XORs it into block counter.
- FUN_0040179d:
  - constructs 8-byte key stream from 0xAABBCCDDEEFF0011

Interpretation:
- Effective per-block keystream = (key64 XOR block_index), bytewise big-endian.
- Encryption and decryption are identical operation due to XOR symmetry.

Why this mattered:
- Enabled direct decryption of hardcoded target bytes into required plaintext password.
