- Semantics map to memcpy/memcmp-like behavior.

Why this led to next step:

  transformed_input[i] = input[i] XOR byte((key64 XOR block_counter), i_mod_8)
  and transformed_input compared to fixed ciphertext,
  the original password is:
  input = ciphertext XOR keystream
- Next step: compute this exactly.
